VerifyEase Sales Channel Integrations
Order and Inventory Management designed to ease your sales.

VerifyEase – as the name implies, it makes life simpler by removing complications and reducing costs. VerifyEase enables the principal (outsourcer) to focus more on customer engagement rather than bothering about vendor-level operational obstacles. VerifyEase is a lightweight and easy to use application that brings down the operational complexities in your verification tasks.

Verification as an activity entails a lot of resources – people, time, money, equipment like printers & scanners, consumables like paper, printer cartridges, etc. People should exhibit a higher level of coordination with constant manual communication to complete the task. It involves generating countless printed forms, capturing the customer data, scanning each of those filled-in forms to enable their digitization, and of course, the cross verification. All these time-consuming processes lead to inefficiencies, time delays for the vendor and increased costs and reduced transparency for the principal.

Out goes this cumbersome process and in comes VerifyEase. A simple, easy to use application (that is also mobile-friendly) removes these inefficiencies and time delays at the ground level, while empowering the principal with transparency and cost reductions.
